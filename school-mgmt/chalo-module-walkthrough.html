01 — Dashboard & Analytics

Real-time school health snapshot for principals and admins

  • 1

    KPI Summary Cards

    Displays total students enrolled, active staff count, today's attendance %, and total fees collected vs outstanding. Auto-refreshed at page load.

  • 2

    Fee Collection Progress

    Visual progress bar showing how much of the expected annual fee revenue has been collected. Color shifts red when below 60%, yellow 60-80%, green above 80%.

  • 3

    Class-wise Attendance Bar Chart

    Horizontal bar chart (Recharts) per class section showing attendance %. Helps principal spot underperforming classes instantly.

  • 4

    Quick Action Shortcuts

    One-click shortcuts to most frequent tasks: Mark Attendance, Generate Challan, View Outstanding, Publish Circular.

  • 5

    Role-based Views

    Principal sees school-wide stats. Teacher sees only their class attendance and timetable. Parent sees their child's data only.

02 — Admission Management

End-to-end enquiry to enrollment pipeline with funnel tracking

  • 1

    Enquiry Registration (Public Form)

    Walk-in parents fill a form: child name, DOB, applying class, parent mobile, source (walk-in / online / referral). No login required. Stored with status = "Enquiry".

  • 2

    Funnel Pipeline View

    5-stage kanban-style funnel: Enquiry → Form Issued → Test Scheduled → Interview → Enrolled. Click any stage card to filter the list below.

  • 3

    Status Advance

    Admin selects a student and advances them to next stage. Each stage change is date-stamped. Notes can be added at each step.

  • 4

    Conversion to Student Record

    On "Enrolled", system auto-creates a full student profile, parent record, assigns to class, and generates admission number.

  • 5

    Enquiry Funnel Analytics

    Summary card shows conversion rate: X enquiries → Y enrollments. Helps management measure marketing effectiveness.

05 — Attendance Tracking

Class-wise student attendance with parent auto-notification

  • 1

    Class Attendance Entry

    Teacher selects class → sees full student roster → marks each student: Present / Absent / Late / On Leave. Saves with timestamp and teacher ID.

  • 2

    Auto WhatsApp Alert on Absence

    When a student is marked absent, parent receives WhatsApp message immediately: "Dear Parent, [Child Name] was marked absent on [Date]. Please contact school if this is an error."

  • 3

    Monthly Attendance Report

    Per student: calendar view showing P/A/L for each day. Total working days, days present, % attendance. Printed in report card if below 75%.

  • 4

    Low Attendance Flag

    Students below 75% attendance are auto-flagged. Principal sees this list on dashboard. System can send WhatsApp notice to parents of flagged students.

  • 5

    Holiday & Working Day Calendar

    Admin defines school holidays, half-days, exam days. Attendance calculation automatically excludes non-working days from denominator.

06 — Timetable & AI Scheduling

AI-powered conflict-free timetable generation + substitution management

  • 1

    Rules Configuration

    Admin sets: number of periods per day, period duration, lunch break position, max continuous periods per subject, preferred morning/afternoon subjects.

  • 2

    AI Auto-Generation (Claude API)

    Click "Auto-Generate" → sends class structure, teacher-subject assignments, and rules to Claude AI → gets back a complete conflict-free weekly schedule in seconds. No teacher double-booked across two classes simultaneously.

  • 3

    Manual Override

    Drag-and-drop interface to adjust individual slots after AI generation. Conflict checker highlights in red if a proposed change creates a clash.

  • 4

    Substitution Management

    Teacher marks absent → system finds available teachers who can cover the period (based on subject compatibility and free periods) → admin approves substitution with one click.

  • 5

    Teacher & Class View

    Each teacher sees their own weekly timetable. Each class sees their schedule. Mobile-friendly view for teachers checking their schedule on phone.

07 — Examination & Report Cards

CBSE-aligned marks entry, AI remarks, and automated report card generation

  • 1

    Exam Schedule Setup

    Admin creates exam (FA1, SA1, FA2, SA2 for CBSE). Sets: exam name, class, subject, date, max marks, passing marks. Visible to teachers on their portal.

  • 2

    Marks Entry by Teacher

    Teacher opens exam → sees student list for their class/subject → enters marks per student. Validates: mark can't exceed max marks. Shows pass/fail instantly.

  • 3

    AI Remark Generation (Claude API)

    Teacher types rough remark: "student is good but needs improvement in math." AI rephrases to professional CBSE language: "Demonstrates consistent effort. Would benefit from focused practice in mathematical problem-solving." 50 remarks in seconds.

  • 4

    CBSE Grade Calculation

    Auto-calculates grade from percentage: A1 (91-100%), A2 (81-90%), B1 (71-80%), B2 (61-70%), C1 (51-60%), C2 (41-50%), D (33-40%), E (below 33%).

  • 5

    Report Card PDF Generation

    Published report cards generate PDF with: school letterhead, student photo, all subjects with marks/grade/remarks, attendance summary, class teacher signature, principal remarks. Secure download link sent to parent.

08 — Fee Management (3-Method System)

Bank challan + counter collection + online payment with AI reconciliation

  • 1

    Fee Structure Setup

    Admin defines fee heads (Tuition, Bus, Lab, Activity, Exam) and amounts per class. Applies to all students in that class/section. Concessions defined separately (e.g., Staff Ward: 50% on tuition).

  • 2

    Method 1: Bank Challan

    Parent pays at bank branch using challan. Admin uploads bank statement CSV. AI reconciliation auto-matches transactions to outstanding challans using amount + date proximity matching.

  • 3

    Method 2: Counter Cash Collection

    Accountant receives cash at school counter → selects student → marks which fee heads are being paid → prints receipt with receipt number, amount, heads paid, date, collector name.

  • 4

    Method 3: Online Payment (Razorpay)

    Parent sees outstanding fees on their portal → clicks Pay Online → Razorpay payment gateway → on success, transaction recorded + WhatsApp receipt sent.

  • 5

    Outstanding Report & Reminders

    Admin sees full outstanding list with days overdue. Auto WhatsApp reminder sent to parents of overdue students. EOD collection report: total collected by method, by fee head, by class.

  • 6

    AI Duplicate Payment Detection

    AI flags cases where same amount paid twice within 7 days for same student. Alert shown to accountant for verification before crediting.

12 — Payroll & Salary

Monthly salary processing with attendance-linked deductions

  • 1

    Salary Structure Definition

    Per staff member: Basic, HRA, TA, DA, Special Allowance (earnings). PF, Professional Tax, LOP (deductions). Gross = sum of earnings. Net = Gross - deductions.

  • 2

    Monthly Payroll Run

    Admin triggers payroll for month → system reads attendance for all staff → calculates LOP deduction for absent days → generates payslip for every active staff member.

  • 3

    Payslip PDF & Distribution

    Payslip PDF with school logo, staff details, earnings/deductions breakdown, net pay. Emailed to staff or available in staff portal. WhatsApp notification with download link.

  • 4

    PF & TDS Register

    Aggregated monthly register for PF contributions and TDS deductions. Exportable for CA/auditor. Annual Form 16 generation (planned).

13 — Reports & Exports

On-demand and scheduled reports for management and compliance

  • 1

    Fee Outstanding Report

    Filterable by class, term, fee head. Shows student name, total due, paid, outstanding, days overdue. Exportable to Excel/PDF. Used for parent meeting preparation.

  • 2

    Attendance Monthly Register

    CBSE format: student names as rows, dates as columns, P/A/L in cells. Auto-calculates % at end. Downloadable as PDF. Required for government inspections.

  • 3

    EOD Cash Collection Report

    End-of-day summary: total cash received at counter, split by fee head, collector-wise, class-wise. Matches accountant's cash drawer for daily reconciliation.

  • 4

    Marksheet & Consolidated Report

    Subject-wise marks for all students in a class for an exam. Rank calculation. Class topper highlighted. Failure list extracted separately.

  • 5

    Automated Scheduled Reports

    Cron jobs: Daily stats at 18:30 (emailed to principal), Weekly fee reminders Monday 08:00 (WhatsApp to parents), Monthly attendance flag report.
